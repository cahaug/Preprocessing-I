I am Cale Haug and I just recently started at the Lambda School. I spent two years at Barrett, the Honors College at Arizona State University, studying Chemistry. I was unable to attend the funeral and mourn properly, and consequently I got very depressed and anhedonic towards my academic duties and personal care. This was a very difficult time, I always had been a star student, but for the first time, that wasn't me. I wasn't happy with the career-direction my schooling was taking me. One of my professors told me Laboratory chemists can expect a ten to twenty year drop in normal life expectancy because of the risks they take with chemical exposure everyday. I stopped going to class, and moved home. I got a job at the local supermarket, fifteen minutes bike from my parent's house. That was two and more years ago now. But the lessons I have learned along the way haven't left me, and I am now a wiser person for my experiences. Computers and developing websites were always hobbies of mine, and now I intend to make them my career. Computers and I definitely get along well.
